About Us

EXCELLENT SECURITY NETWORK SDN BHD

Ditubuhkan pada 22 Februari 2001, EXCELLENT SECURITY NETWORK SDN BHD (Co. No. 539992-P) menyediakan perkhidmatan keselamatan komprehensif untuk sektor industri, komersial dan kediaman. Dibangunkan oleh bekas pegawai polis & pemimpin industri, syarikat kami menekankan profesionalisme, latihan dan kawalan kualiti.

Mission

Menyediakan perkhidmatan keselamatan menyeluruh dengan kakitangan yang berkemahiran, jujur dan profesional.

Vision

Meningkatkan standard industri keselamatan dan memberikan perkhidmatan yang efisien kepada pelanggan.

Objectives
  • Pengambilan dan latihan pengawal berkualiti (termasuk ex-police/ex-army).
  • Kawalan kualiti & pasukan pengawasan di premis klien.
  • Ketersediaan pengurusan untuk rundingan & tindakan pantas terhadap aduan.
  • Perlindungan insurans liabiliti dan indemniti profesional.

Our Expertise

Type of Services

Internal Static Guards

Unarmed security guards for factories, condominiums, construction sites, commercial and residential complexes. Trained in first aid and firefighting.

Armed Guards (Ex-Police / Ex-Army)

Armed personnel assigned to banks, goldsmiths, warehouses and high-risk premises. Regular shooting/practical training provided twice a month.

Guard Dogs Unit

Police-trained canine units with handlers for perimeter prevention and patrol surveillance for warehouses, estates and enclosed properties.

Private Investigation

Investigations into commercial crime, IP infringement and background screening handled confidentially by experienced investigators.

Cash-in-Transit (CIT)

Armoured vehicles and armed escort teams for secure transportation of cash and valuables. Charges vary by distance and risk.

Bodyguard & Escort Services

Plain-clothes armed protection for VIPs and executives, threat assessment and tailored personal security plans.

Response & Patrol Units

Mobile patrol units (ex-police/ex-army) for periodic surveillance, watchman supervision and rapid response.

Central Monitoring System (CMS)

24-hour monitoring to supplement on-ground officers and minimize damage during incidents via field transmitters and HQ coordination.

Training & Consultancy

Comprehensive training (shooting, safety, fire safety) and consultancy for clients wishing to build or improve in-house security.
